Note

Why these exist

Most of what I build solves a real problem for someone I know. The job search agent runs in my house. Milo runs on my team. Linda writes my Slack messages. They aren't demos.

I'm publishing them because the patterns travel — once you've seen one Gmail-label-to-Notion-digest agent, you can build five. The hard part isn't the code. It's deciding what's worth scoring and what to ignore.

Every page here will tell you the parts that are easy to copy and the parts I had to think hard about. Use what's useful. Skip the rest.
